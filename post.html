Green Jade Games receives further backing from Yolo Investments

18 May 2021 Back to news

Yolo Investments has once again backed innovative casino content suppliers Green Jade Games, providing additional funding to further power the company’s continued growth.

Yolo Investments first purchased equity in Green Jade Games in 2019, and has now expanded its position in the supplier following an above expectations performance.

The new funding will help Green Jade Games continue its game-changing mission within the casino space, where its arcade-style and skill-based content continues to disrupt and win plaudits.

Yolo Investments GP Tim Heath said: “Green Jade Games has very quickly become one of the most exciting young games developers our industry has seen in many years. The team’s innate understanding of casino content has enabled them to create innovative new concepts that resonate with players. This is why we are delighted to increase our stake in the company as it continues to go from strength to strength.”

Green Jade Games CEO: Benedict McDonagh said: “Yolo Investments has, from day one, been more than a funding partner. Their complementary experiences within the industry are shared in a succinct and professional manner, led by the passion and expertise of Tim and his team. As our vision comes to fruition, their additional investment cements our opportunity to deliver revolutionary product, without compromise.”

Company produces green energy and offers secure and affordable accommodation for crypto miners

Greenbit.Energy provides a revolutionary solution to the environmental impact of Bitcoin mining, by generating reusable wind and solar energy and using it to mine the popular cryptocurrency.

Operating on the small Estonian island of Saaremaa, the company produces green energy and offers secure and affordable accommodation for crypto miners. Currently, it operates three windmills with a total output of 2mw/h and a solar energy harvesting plant of 0.53 mw/h.

Its Bitcoin miners are held within a specifically modified transport container, designed to achieve maximum energy efficiency via heating protocols and a state-of-the-art air filtration system.

With people increasingly concerned about the environmental consequences of Bitcoin mining, Greenbit.Energy is showing there is a sustainable way forward for the cryptocurrency world.
